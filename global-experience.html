Global Experience

Thailand

2018 - Present

Continued my musical development in Bangkok, initially self-taught before studying with Irina Novikova. Performed with Bangkok Charity Orchestra and won multiple international competitions while representing Thailand.

Nepal

2017-2018

Faced unique challenges finding playable pianos but maintained high-level practice through self-teaching. Gave a memorable solo recital in Kathmandu with over 200 attendees at the French Ambassador's residence.

India

2016-2017

Experienced tough living conditions but continued practicing piano at a high level. These challenging experiences taught resilience and adaptability in pursuing musical excellence.

China

2007 - 2015

Born in China and began my musical journey at age 5. Studied in Chengdu for my first three years of piano, then moved to Beijing where I trained with Tong Shen at The Affiliated High School of China Conservatory of Music.

International Performances

2020 - Present

Performed across the globe including Berlin, Prague, San Francisco, Hong Kong, Moscow, Shanghai, and Bangkok. Many Carnegie Hall invitations (postponed due to COVID-19 pandemic).
